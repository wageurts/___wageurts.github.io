What is or what are JWT's?

JSON Web Tokens are an open, industry standard RFC 7159 method for representing claims securely between two parties.

Claims..?!?!

Claims are statements about an entity (typically, the user) and additional metadata.

Different type of claims

  1. Resevered claims. (eg. iss, exp)
  2. Public claims. -> IANA JWT reg.
  3. Private claims.
